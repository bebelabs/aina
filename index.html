Scholarship Program

Investing in the next generation of AI-native accounting professionals

The AI Native Accounting Scholarship supports students and early-career professionals who are passionate about transforming the accounting profession through technology.

Program Overview

  • Annual scholarship awards for accounting students pursuing AI and technology integration
  • Mentorship opportunities with industry leaders featured on our podcast
  • Access to exclusive AI Native Accounting community events and resources
  • Internship connections with leading AI-forward accounting firms
  • Professional development stipends for AI and technology certifications

Eligibility

We welcome applications from undergraduate and graduate students pursuing degrees in accounting, finance, or related fields who demonstrate a genuine interest in AI and technology's role in transforming the profession. Candidates should show academic excellence, leadership potential, and a vision for how they'll contribute to AI-native accounting.

Application Process

Applications open annually in January. Candidates submit their academic records, a personal statement on their vision for AI in accounting, and a brief video introducing themselves and their goals. Finalists are interviewed by our selection committee, which includes past podcast guests and industry leaders.
